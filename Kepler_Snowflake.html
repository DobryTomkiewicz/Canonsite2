Snowflake

by Kepler

Commentary

Kepler considers applying questions of geometry to answering the question of why snowflakes have 6 sides. Does he come to the correct answer? Not even close. Apparently the cause of snowflake’s shape was only known recently, and it requires knowledge molecular thermodynamics to explain. In kepler’s time the atom only existed as a subject of philosophical debate, similar to modern debates about the correct interpretation of Planck’s constant.
