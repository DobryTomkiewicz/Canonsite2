Author

Claudius Ptolemy

Biography

History remembers Ptolemy as a great deceiver who convinced the world of geocentrism for a millennium and a charlatan astrologer. However, he also recorded several advances in trigonometry and left an extremely positive mark on mathematics. For this he is worth reading for those who are also interested in Archimedes and Apollonius.
