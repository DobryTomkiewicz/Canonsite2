Author

Francis Bacon

Biography

Often considered "The Godfather of the Scientific Method." This is despite him contributing nothing to science and scientists like William Gilbert, Johannes Kepler, and others existing before him. The more I read about this guy the more I feel inclined to lump him in with "Philosophers of Science" like Popper. Still, he's expected to show up in lists like these, but I can't help but feel he only gets credit for ideas had by others.
