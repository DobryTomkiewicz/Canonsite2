De Practica Geometrie

by Fibonacci

Commentary

Fibonacci provides a work that is meant to serve as both an introduction to Euclid's Elements and a guide to the practical applications of geometry. This includes simple area problems for farming. Essentially, an early work of field theory but rather than quantum field theory or electromagnetic field theory this is a work of corn field theory.
