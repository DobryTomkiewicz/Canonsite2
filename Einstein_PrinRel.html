Commentary

Many lists count “Relativity: The Special and General Theory” as the essential work by Einstein to read. This is incorrect. That work is not Einstein’s significant contribution, but a slightly technical “Pop science” version of his theories intended to amuse the general public. His actual significant papers are presented in the English in the volume “The Principle of Relativity.” This is the book to read if you want to actually look at what Einstein did to deserve his reputation.
