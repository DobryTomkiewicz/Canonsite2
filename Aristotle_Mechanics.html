Mechanics

by Aristotle

Commentary

It’s an open question as to whether or not this is a genuine work of Aristotle. It’s a bit disorganized in it’s approach and very short. However it is still significant as Galileo spends a significant portion of his Dialogues on Two New Sciences to responding to it and resolving the “Wheel Paradox” that Aristotle presents.

Translations

  • This can be found in the Oxford Translation of the Complete Works of Aristotle, or the ebook Delphi Classics series. The Delphi classics version is slightly superior as it contains figures.
