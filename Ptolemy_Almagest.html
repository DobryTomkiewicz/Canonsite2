Almagest

by Ptolemy

Commentary

Although portrayed as the villain in most stories of the history of science, Ptolemy deserves credit for his skill as a mathematician. The term “Almagest” roughly translates to “The Greatest” and it is not difficult to see why. It is easily the most mathematically developed physical treatise from antiquity, with clever innovations on both Euclid’s and Apollonius’s geometry.
