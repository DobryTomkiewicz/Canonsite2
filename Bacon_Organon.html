New Organon

by Bacon

Commentary

Supposedly the start of the scientific method. Now, We’ve seen several scientific works before this. Although we’ve all been taught some version of the Baconian method in middle school, it is worth noting that this was not without controversy. Newton, in the concluding General Scholium to the Principia Mathematica, states “Experimental Philosophy has no room for Hypothesis.”
